Academic Excellence

Board Success

0%

Bharathi Vidhyaa Kendhra has consistently achieved 100% in board exams, maintaining an exceptional academic record. High achievers in both the ICSE and ISC streams have been produced by the school, demonstrating its strong academic rigor and efficient teaching strategies.

ISC Stream 2025

0%

The first batch of students in the ISC stream 2025 achieved remarkable results, with the school topper scoring 97.5%, including two centum in Physics and Computer Science. With top scores in Chemistry and Biology, the second topper attained 95.25%, demonstrating excellence in all scientific fields.

ICSE Stream

0%

The school topper in the ICSE stream, scored 95%, followed closely by the second topper, scoring 94.8%. Additionally, students demonstrated academic strength in both language and humanities subjects by achieving centum scores in English and History.

Milestones Through the Years

2015

Teach a Man to Fish

BVK launched the “Teach a Man to Fish” initiative, London, focusing on entrepreneurial skill-building among students.

2016

ISA Recognition

BVK introduced the ISA – a cross-cultural exchange program, broadening global exposure and cultural understanding.

2017

Curriculum Expansion

The Cambridge Young Learners Curriculum was introduced for younger students to strengthen their English communication skills. To further enhance literacy, the school also launched the Literacy Chronicle.

2018

Tiruppur Stars Award

BVK hosted the Tiruppur Stars Award, an event focused to pick talented young learners of Tiruppur.

2025

Opening of the new Administrative Block, enhancing facilities and operational efficiency.

2026

Recreational Development
