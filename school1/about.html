About Us

Nurturing Excellence Since 1995


    Bharathi Vidhyaa Kendhra- an extended hub of Bharathi Kids Kshethralaya, Tiruppur, aims to bring a paradigm shift to 21 century education which is bold, breaks the mould, flexible, creative, challenging, complex and comprehensive. We are committed to helping our learners reach their full potential. Our curriculum has been ear marked for outcome - based, focusing on what learners know and can do by designing lessons based on upper levels of Bloom’s taxonomy - synthesis, analysis and application research - driven active learners work collaboratively in a global classroom.


    Vision :

Our vision is to enable young minds to think, to create situations to learn and build a purpose in life.
SHAKTHI : We believe deeply in the transformative power of education because great education can transcend everything.
INVATI : Innovation is a prerequisite for survival... We make sure our students have the right attitude towards change and challenge the norms.
SIDDHI : Most people know what to do, they just don’t do what they know. Achievement flows from discipline, which requires strength. Mental strength comes first,
                we take every thought captive and make each one serve the purpose.

    Mission :

BVK provides an excellent, learner centered education through inspirational teaching and empowerment of all pupils to reach full potential, intellectually, physically, socially and morally in a happy, caring, stimulating environment promoting self- esteem and confidence.

Igniting minds, fostering excellence. Our community thrives on academic and personal growth.
