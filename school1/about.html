Our Story

Bharathi Vidhyaa Kendhra — an extended hub of Bharathi Kids Kshethralaya, Tiruppur, aims to bring a paradigm shift to 21st-century education which is bold, breaks the mould, flexible, creative, challenging, complex, and comprehensive.

We are committed to helping our learners reach their full potential. Our curriculum has been earmarked for outcome-based learning, focusing on what learners know and can do by designing lessons based on upper levels of Bloom’s taxonomy — synthesis, analysis, and application research-driven active learners work collaboratively in a global classroom.
